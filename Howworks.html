How ReWear Works

Simple steps to start swapping and earning points for sustainable fashion

1

List Your Items

Upload photos and details of clothes you no longer wear. Add them to your profile for others to discover.

2

Swap or Redeem

Choose to swap directly with other members or use points to redeem items you love.

3

Confirm the Exchange

Agree on swap details with your exchange partner and arrange for the handoff.

4

Make an Impact

Every swap reduces textile waste and promotes sustainable fashion practices.
